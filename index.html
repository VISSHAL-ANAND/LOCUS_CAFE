Our Story

More than a café.
A gathering place.

Tucked into the heart of Coimbatore on Avinashi Road, Cafe Locus is where the aroma of freshly baked croissants meets the quiet hum of a city slowing down. We craft every plate and pour with intent.

From our V60 pour-overs to our handmade pâtisserie, we believe good food is a form of care — for the people we serve, and the craft we practise.
